Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How quickly can you start working on my project?

Our typical response time is within 24 hours of receiving your inquiry. For most projects, we can begin within 3-5 business days after agreement. Rush projects may be accommodated depending on current workload.

What's your typical pricing structure?

We offer both fixed-price projects and hourly rates depending on the scope of work. Digital marketing campaigns typically start at $1,500/month, web development projects at $3,000, and content creation at $500/month. Request a custom quote for precise pricing.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we regularly work with clients worldwide. Our team is distributed across multiple time zones, allowing us to accommodate clients in different regions. All communication and contracts are in English, and we accept payments in multiple currencies.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have extensive experience in technology/SaaS, e-commerce, professional services, healthcare, and education sectors. However, we've successfully served clients across 20+ industries and can quickly adapt to new verticals.

How do you ensure project quality?

All projects go through our 3-step quality assurance process: 1) Specialist review, 2) Peer review, and 3) Client approval stage. We also provide regular progress updates and maintain open communication channels throughout the project lifecycle.
